Intelli-Fleet Services

Intellifleet is a software system that acts as platform for communication between couriers and customers that seek courier services. The target market is large enterprise business companies that need to transport big shipments.

Client Dashboard

Clients can request for packages to be collected and delivered. Check Collection and delivery status. View Financial statements.

Optimsed Routing

Provides truck drivers with optimised routes to travel while collecting and delivering packages.

Fleet Management Dashboard

Provides information about current vehicles on the system and their status, vehicle tracking capabilities and management of drivers. View business status as well as past and current perfomance comparisons.
